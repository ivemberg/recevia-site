Features

Built for practical trip expense tracking.

Recevia focuses on the core workflow: create a trip, capture receipts, review the detected data, and export a clean report without adding unnecessary complexity.

✈️

Create and manage trips

Keep trip names, dates, receipts, totals, and archived records organized in one place.

🧾

Capture and review receipts

Import from camera or photo library, then edit merchant, amount, date, notes, and category when needed.

📄

Export polished PDFs

Generate professional reports ready to share with accounting, managers, or reimbursement workflows.

Geographic coverage

Designed for receipts captured across international business trips.

Recevia is built for travel receipts collected on the road, including international itineraries. The app supports receipts and OCR heuristics for common English, Italian, and East Asian layouts, including yen-based receipts captured in Japan.

Worldwide trip usage

Use it wherever your trip takes you

Recevia is not limited to one city or one country. You can create trips, store receipts, and export reports for domestic and international travel from the same app.

Currency-aware OCR

Handles multiple receipt formats

The app currently includes heuristics for currencies and formats such as EUR, USD, JPY, CNY, KRW, SGD, HKD, and TWD, with fallback to manual correction when OCR is imperfect.

How it works

Simple, local-first, and ready to share.

Local-first storage

Trips, receipt metadata, OCR text, and receipt images stay on your device first. When available, iCloud can mirror them across devices signed into the same Apple Account.

On-device OCR support

Recevia analyzes receipt images on-device to help detect merchants, dates, totals, and categories before you review the result manually.
